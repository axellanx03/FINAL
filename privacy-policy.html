Privacy Policy for Kaffee-In

Effective Date: November 2024

At Kaffee-In, your privacy matters to us! We’re dedicated to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard it. Whether you’re visiting to browse our menu, place an order, or just explore, we want you to feel confident and secure.

Here’s everything you need to know about how we handle your data:

1. What Information We Collect and Why

We collect certain information to improve your experience, process your orders, and keep things running smoothly. Here’s what we may gather:

a. Personal Information (You Provide)

  • Name: So we know who you are when you place an order or contact us.
  • Email Address: To send order confirmations, updates, or special offers (only if you opt in).
  • Phone Number: In case we need to contact you about an order or reservation.
  • Address: If you’re ordering for delivery.

b. Payment Information

We use secure third-party processors to handle your payment details. We don’t store your payment information directly, but rest assured it’s kept safe.

c. Browsing Information (Automatically Collected)

  • IP Address & Device Info: Helps us understand how you’re using our site and improve it.
  • Cookies: These little data files help us remember your preferences, like keeping items in your cart.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Fulfill Orders and Reservations: To make sure your coffee or meal gets to you without a hitch.
  • Improve Our Services: By analyzing how you interact with the site, we can make it better and more user-friendly.
  • Send Updates & Promotions: If you’ve signed up, we’ll occasionally send you special offers, discounts, and updates about what’s brewing at Kaffee-In.

3. Do We Share Your Information?

We don’t sell your data—ever. But to provide you with the best service, we may share it with trusted partners:

  • Payment Providers: To process your transactions securely.
  • Delivery Services: If you’re getting something delivered.
  • Analytics Tools: To help us understand website traffic and improve our site.

4. Our Use of Cookies

Cookies are like digital sticky notes that help us remember your preferences. We use them to:

  • Keep your cart full if you leave and come back later.
  • Understand what’s popular on our site so we can serve you better.

5. How We Keep Your Information Safe

Your security is a top priority. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. While no system is completely foolproof, we’re constantly working to keep your information safe.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You’re in control of your data, and you have the right to:

  • Access or Update Your Information.
  • Delete Your Information.
  • Opt Out of Marketing: You can unsubscribe from our emails anytime.

7. Links to Other Sites

Sometimes, we may link to other websites. Just remember, once you leave Kaffee-In, we’re not responsible for how those sites handle your data.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website isn’t intended for children under 13, and we don’t knowingly collect their data. If we learn that we have, we’ll delete it right away.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we do, we’ll post the new policy here and update the effective date.
